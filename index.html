Overview

What Are Restaurants?

A restaurant is a place where people can eat and dine in. There are many types of restaurants, ranging from fast food to family ones. Over time, Restaurants can get more popular and popular, Which leads into new innovations and technology to help with the efficiency. This website will look into the different types of restaurants and innovations developed today.

Categories

When going to a restaurant you can have Order/Take-out or Dine-in
Order/Take-out

Ordering food means to buy food online and having it delivered to you.

Take-out means to buy food online but you go in person to pick it up.

Dine-in

Dine-in means going to the restaurant in person, But having a seat and ordering food there. Your food is brought to you and you basically eat in the restaurant.
