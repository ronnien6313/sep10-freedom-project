What Are Restaurants?

Categories

When going to a restaurant you can have Order/Take-out or Dine-in
Order/Take-out

Ordering food means to buy food online and having it delivered to you.

Take-out means to buy food online but you go in person to pick it up.

Dine-in

Dine-in means going to the restaurant in person, But having a seat and ordering food there.

Your food is brought to you and you basically eat in the restaurant.

Order Kiosk

Order Kiosk's are alternatives for ordering food in the store. Instead of waiting on a line you can order on a kiosk.

It is most commonly found in most fast food places like Mc Donalds.

Kiosk's are great for efficiency of ordering since it's technically taking more orders in.

More orders also means more work on the workers, This is a con of Order Kiosks.

Automated Delivery Car

The Automated Delivery Car can hold up to 20 pounds of food and make a 4 mile round trip

The car can't be stolen from because it has a lock, the only way of opening this lock is when the client gets a link from the app.

The car has a 360 view of it's surroundings, allowing it to go around obstacles and walk through crosswalks safely.

Incase it gets stolen, There is a tracker on the car for insurance, But through numerous testing there it was never stolen.

The car is still in development and testing and so far it can only travel short distances, But this innovation in itself is an amazing technological feat.
